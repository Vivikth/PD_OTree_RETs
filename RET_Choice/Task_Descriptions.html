Tabulation Tasks

Task Description: Subjects must use their mathematical abilities to tabulate quantities

  • Level 1: Quantities are binary.
  • Level 2: Quantities are decimal.
  • Level 3: Quantities may be non-numeric.
  • Level 4: Quantities may be foreign.

Concealment Tasks

Task Description: Subjects must use their analytical capabilities to conceal content

  • Level 1: Content is simple.
  • Level 2: Content is alphabetic.
  • Level 3: Content is alphanumeric.
  • Level 4: Content is non-alphanumeric.

Interpretation Tasks

Task Description: Subjects must use their visual abilities to interpret signs.

  • Level 1: Signs are numeric.
  • Level 2: Signs are alphabetic.
  • Level 3: Signs are foreign.
  • Level 4: Signs are non-alphanumeric.

Replication Tasks

Task Description: Subjects must use their technological abilities to replicate items.

  • Level 1: There are only 5 different items
  • Level 2: Items are English
  • Level 3: Items are alphanumeric
  • Level 4: Items are non-alphanumeric

Organisation Tasks

Task Description: Subjects must use their categorization abilities to rearrange items.

  • Level 1: There are only 5 different items
  • Level 2: Items are English
  • Level 3: Items are alphanumeric
  • Level 4: Items may be non-alphanumeric
